🧠 Section 1: Getting Started with AI Tools

💬 ChatGPT Basics

Think of ChatGPT like your always-available, tech-savvy sidekick. Whether you’re troubleshooting FPS drops or figuring out BIOS weirdness, this AI has your back — if you know how to talk to it.

🚀 Getting Set Up

Starting is super simple:

Step What to Do
1Visit chat.openai.com
2Create an account and verify your email
3Choose between Free and Plus plans
4Start asking questions like:
“Why is my game stuttering even though my FPS is stable?”

💸 Free vs Paid Versions

Plan Description
🆓 Free (GPT-3.5) Great for basic tasks, quick fixes, and brainstorming — still powerful, just a bit limited with deeper tech prompts.
💎 ChatGPT Plus (GPT-4) Handles multi-layered troubleshooting like a pro. Understands nuance, remembers context longer, and gives more accurate answers.

🛠 Pro Tip: Use clear, specific prompts. The more context you give it, the better it performs. And yep — you can ask it to help you write or rewrite prompts, too.
