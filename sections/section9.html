🧰 Section 9: Companion Toolkit Overview

This section highlights the bonus digital tools bundled with your guide — all designed to make optimizing, tracking, and customizing your gaming experience easier.

📂 What’s Included

  • System Change Tracker — A log to track hardware changes, software installs, settings edits, and maintenance tasks.
  • FPS Performance Sheet — Record before/after FPS stats, settings changes, and improvements.
  • Optimization Checklist — A printable checklist with space for notes, recommended tools, and reminders.
  • Prompt Planner — A structured doc to help you craft effective ChatGPT prompts and track useful responses.

🧪 Prompt Example

I’ve just installed a new GPU and updated my drivers. Help me log and test performance differences using the System Change Tracker and FPS Sheet in the Companion Toolkit.

📝 How to Use the Toolkit

  • Track changes as you go — Log even small tweaks so you can reverse mistakes easily.
  • Use the FPS sheet regularly — Record your game version, drivers, temps, and FPS after each change.
  • Reference past fixes — A great setup you did once can be replicated — if it’s logged.
  • Craft better prompts — Write what worked and refine what didn’t.

💡 Pro Tip: Think of this like your own user manual for your system — the more you log, the less guesswork later.

✨ Bonus Tip: Let ChatGPT Help You Use the Toolkit

You don’t have to fill everything out alone. ChatGPT can actually help you update, summarize, and manage your Companion Toolkit files.

🧾 Toolkit Contents Recap

  • System Change Tracker: Log hardware, software, and config changes.
  • FPS Log Sheet: Record game, settings, FPS before/after tweaks.
  • Optimization Checklist: Follow and update a performance-focused checklist.
  • Prompt Planner: Keep track of AI prompts that worked well for you.

⚙️ Upload to ChatGPT & Ask:

  1. Open ChatGPT and upload one of your toolkit files (PDF, spreadsheet, etc.).
  2. Ask ChatGPT to analyze, summarize, or even update the file for you.
  3. Use natural language. For example:
Here’s my FPS tracker sheet. Can you highlight the tweaks that gave me the biggest FPS gains?
I’m uploading my system change tracker. Can you summarize this month’s upgrades and what I forgot to log?

💡 Pro Tip: Use the Prompt Planner to document which AI queries helped the most — and reuse them later!
